Writing hooks is a very personalized process based on the campaign and your target audience. No AI tools can give you a best hook line, but just a basic understanding of its flow and structure.

Here's how you can create hooks
1) Write a hook
2) Peer- test among your colleagues
3) Implement it in your strategy
4) AB test continuously using different alteration based on the data you obtained

There is no silver bullets to master hook, it's all about implementation, readjusting and iteration.
